# Startup Funding Routes in Saudi Arabia

Founders frequently conflate three different funding routes. They are distinct:

| Route | Type | Provider | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| SVC partner funds | Equity | SVC via partner VCs | SVC does not invest directly in startups |
| Monshaat programmes | Support | Monshaat | Incubation, mentorship, training |
| Kafalah | Debt | Banks, guaranteed by Kafalah | Loan guarantee, not a grant |

No route guarantees funding; each assesses applications on its own terms, and no
specific venture fund should be named as "the best."

## Where to go

- Equity: SVC's public partner-fund list at https://www.svc.com.sa/en, and
  MAGNiTT (https://magnitt.com) for MENA VC benchmarks.
- Support: Monshaat at https://www.monshaat.gov.sa/en.
- Debt: a Kafalah-participating bank.

## Next step

Start with the Monshaat programme list at https://www.monshaat.gov.sa/en and
match the route to your stage.
