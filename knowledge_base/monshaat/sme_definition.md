# SME Definitions and Classifications

Monshaat — the Small and Medium Enterprises General Authority — classifies
enterprises by size band (micro, small, medium) based on headcount and revenue
thresholds. The size band determines which support programmes a business can
access and is relevant to hiring obligations.

## Why the size band matters

- Eligibility for specific Monshaat programmes can depend on the band.
- Saudization (Nitaqat) hiring expectations apply by sector and company-size
  band; current rules are published by HRSD at https://hrsd.gov.sa/en.
