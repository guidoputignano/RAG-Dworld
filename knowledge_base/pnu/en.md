## Talent Acquisition Portal

This service enables talented students to apply for specific fields online and track/modify their applications.​

Students

[Service details](service-details.aspx?eti=196)
[Start Service](https://eservice.pnu.edu.sa/apps/talented-women/main/start-apply  )

## University ID Inquiry

This service enables
university students to inquire about their university ID. It allows newly
admitted students to find out their ID number in case they did not receive the
SMS containing their academic information. It also provides the option to
request a password for their university email.​

Students

[Service details](service-details.aspx?eti=197)
[Start Service](https://app.pnu.edu.sa/StudentNoSearch/Home.aspx  )

## Change of Major Request

This service allows admitted students to request a change to another major. The portal will only display the majors that meet the student's eligibility criteria (cumulative score) for admission.​

Students

[Service details](service-details.aspx?eti=198)
[Start Service](https://dar.pnu.edu.sa/darms/Default.aspx  )

## Withdrawal from Admission

This service allows newly admitted students to request withdrawal from admission electronically, without the need to visit the university in person.​

Students

[Service details](service-details.aspx?eti=199)
[Start Service](https://eservice.pnu.edu.sa/admissionwithdrawal  )

## Issuance of University ID

This service allows
newly admitted students to schedule for issuing their university ID and to take
the placement test.​

Students

[Service details](service-details.aspx?eti=200)
[Start Service](https://op.pnu.edu.sa/cardsv2/  )
