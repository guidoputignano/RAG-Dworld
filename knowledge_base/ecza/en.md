## Facilitating the next generation of investment opportunities at the heart of the global economy

![vision 3030](/sites/default/files/styles/large/public/2021-06/Group%203%402x.png.webp?itok=eUBP4rK5)

The Economic Cities and Special Zones Authority (ECZA) is the umbrella regulator of Saudi Arabia’s Economic Cities (ECs) and Special Economic Zones (SEZs).

Each zone is uniquely designed to unlock key segments of Saudi Arabia’s economic potential, with ECZA providing a safe and modern business environment.

![MBS](/sites/default/files/styles/big_bg_sm/public/2022-01/mbs.jpg.webp?h=e771e0d2&itok=I4YwjqYG)

##### Empowered by the full support of the Kingdom’s leadership

“We shall take into account the comparative advantages of the Kingdom’s different regions, assess their feasibility for promising sectors, and then establish special zones, each with a specific focus on logistics, tourism, industry, or finance. Special regulations to boost investment possibilities and diversify government revenues will be applied in these zones."
