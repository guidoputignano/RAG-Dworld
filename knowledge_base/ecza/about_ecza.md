## About ECZA

## Saudi Arabia’s Economic Regulator Cities and Special Economic Zones’ Umbrella

The Economic Cities and Special Zones (ECZA) is the enabler and the umbrella regulator of Saudi Arabia’s Economic Cities (ECs) and Special Economic Zones (SEZs). ECZA was originally formed in 2010 to oversee the development of ECs.

In 2019, ECZA’s mandate was expanded to include the supervision of SEZs and the creation of the right regulatory environment to attract businesses. We are launching SEZs to capitalize on Saudi Arabia’s potential as a global business hub. They will play a major role in achieving Saudi Arabia’s economic development goals under Vision 2030.

![Why ecza calmer BG-min.jpg](/sites/default/files/styles/big_bg_sm/public/2022-01/Why%20ecza%20calmer%20BG-min.jpg.webp?h=471c725f&itok=C_p79gzj)

### Our vision

To be the umbrella regulator and trusted national reference for Economic Cities and Special Economic Zones with global standards.

### Our mission

To raise the Kingdom’s global competitiveness through
the launch and enablement of an ECs and SEZs ecosystem
that provides:

An investor-friendly regulatory environment, competitive
sector-specific incentives and enablers.

Highly-efficient integrated government services.

An inspiring living experience with global quality of life
standards.

## Our value proposition

![](/sites/default/files/2021-10/noun_Venn%20Diagram_3598400.svg)

#### Strategic Alignment and Strategic Interoperability Integration

Aligning with national strategies and ensuring strategic integration and coordination among ECs and SEZs.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-10/noun_buildings_2250918.svg)

#### Integration and Interoperability with base economy

Ensuring strategic and interoperability of SEZs and ECs with the base economy for local market protection.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-10/noun_chart_2444642_0.svg)

#### Regulatory environment, exemptions, and incentives

Supporting SEZs in assessing and obtaining appropriate exemptions and incentive packages and developing an optimal regulatory environment for investors.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-10/noun_Government_1803489%20%281%29.svg)

#### Integrated Government Services

Supporting and providing efficient, integrated government services to ensure sustainability and ease of doing business.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-10/noun_Globe_2444628.svg)

#### Assessment of strategic performance

Applying standard and unified mechanisms for evaluating of and licensing SEZs, and monitoring their strategic performance.

![](/sites/default/files/2021-10/noun_Government_1803489%20%281%29.svg)

#### Vocal point for receiving all SEZs applications

Apply standardized comprehensive methodology to raise to the Council of Minister for approval

## Our Economic Cities ecosystem governance model

As the umbrella regulator, ECZA creates the perfect regulatory framework and facilitates the Master Developer to build and operate the ECs. We form an integrated partnership with the Master Developer, providing efficient integrated government services, and assist with investment attraction activities.

![ecen](/sites/default/files/inline-images/EC%20English%20Master%20Dev_0.png)

## Our Special Economic Zones ecosystem model

As the enabler and umbrella regulator, ECZA creates the regulatory framework and facilitates the Relevant Entity to build and operate the SEZs.

###### We serve our ambitious mandate via six ecosystem components:

* Strategies, SEZ evaluation and licensing
* Incentives and exemptions
* Framework and guidance
* Regulations
* Interoperability
* Performance management

## Our Products

**Our two unique products:**

* **Special Economic Zones** - A Special Economic Zone is a geographically delimited area that facilitates specific economic activities by providing competitive advantages and legislative frameworks that differ from the base economy, while complying with the Kingdom’s obligations under international treaties.

* **Economic Cities** - ECs are full fledge cities that are funded, developed and operated by the private sector under government oversight and enablement. ECs offer various and comprehensive businesses (Industrial & Services), residential and entertainment solutions with elevated standards.
