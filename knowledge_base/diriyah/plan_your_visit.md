#### Bujairi Terrace Free Access Pass & Valet Service

Last updated: 3 May 2026, 12:48 Riyadh (GMT+03)Dear Visitors,
We like to inform you, access to visit Bujairi Terrace will be complimentary starting from Friday, May 1, 2026, until Wednesday, September 30, 2026. Guests who spend 150 SAR on dining experiences in Bujairi Terrace and Zallal, will be eligible to receive free valet service.
Be sure to ask your restaurant about the service during your unapparelled dining experience.
Kindly note that this offer applies exclusively for restaurants only. Please ensure to validate valet parking with respected restaurants before car is requested for pick up.
