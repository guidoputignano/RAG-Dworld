H.E. Fahad Al-Saif – Minister of Investment – leads a multidisciplinary team and oversees the national investment ecosystem, the development of the environment, and strategic directions. He also supports the Saudi private sector’s investment orientations outside the Kingdom and works on attracting investments into various priority, vital, and promising sectors.
He is also focused on the growth of the private sector in the Kingdom, which is considered a main driver of Saudi Vision 2030 and an important partner in the plan to diversify the Kingdom’s economic resources.

— His Excellency Mr Fahad bin Abduljalil Al-Saif
