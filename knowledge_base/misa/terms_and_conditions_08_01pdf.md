The applicant is committed to the following :

Guide.
4. Complete the annual update required for registration in accordance with the provisions of the
Executive Regulations of the Investment Law and the Investor Guide.
5. Refrain from conducting any excluded activity without obtaining the necessary approvals in
accordance with the Executive Regulations of the Investment Law and the Investor Guide.

in accordance with the laws and regulations in force in the Kingdom to complete the service request
procedures on behalf of the applicant (the original) .

accordance with the provisions of the Executive Regulations of the Investment Law and the
Investor Guide.
