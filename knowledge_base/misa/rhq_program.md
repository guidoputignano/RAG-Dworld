# Regional Headquarters (RHQ) Programme

The RHQ Programme encourages multinational groups to base their regional
headquarters in Saudi Arabia. It is aimed at investors with a multi-country
regional mandate rather than single-market operators.

## Mandatory functions

An RHQ must perform a set of strategic and management functions for the region
(for example regional strategy, business planning, and coordination of group
entities). The full list of mandatory and optional functions is in the RHQ
Investor Manual (PDF-03).

## Incentives

The programme offers tax-related incentives for qualifying regional
headquarters. Specific rates and the precise incentive package depend on current
policy and are published by MISA — this guide does not quote rates.

## Eligibility

RHQ eligibility is assessed individually by MISA. This page describes the
programme; it does not confirm whether any particular company qualifies.
