## EXPLORE KAFD

Previous

![](https://opti.kafd.sa/globalassets/images/cards/slides/pif-image-website-011958.jpg)

#### PIF TOWER

Designed to evoke the Kingdom’s desert crystals, the 80-storey PIF Tower pierces the clouds at 385 meters high and features 185,000 sqm of meticulously organized office spaces, dining areas and an auditorium.

![](https://opti.kafd.sa/globalassets/images/cards/slides/conference-center-20cc4.png)

#### CONFERENCE CENTER

Stretched over an area of 30,000 sqm, the Conference Center is among the largest of its kind in Riyadh. Blending aesthetic excellence with functionality, it is an ideal location for an array of events.

![](https://opti.kafd.sa/globalassets/images/cards/slides/kafd-metro-stationa664.jpg)

#### KAFD METRO STATION

KAFD's striking metro station is a main stop on the Riyadh metro, providing access to key locations around the city. These 45,000 sqm innovative structure was designed by Zaha Hadid.

![](https://opti.kafd.sa/globalassets/images/cards/slides/financial-plaza-295f4.jpg)

#### FINANCIAL PLAZA

A business district, offering 500,000 sqm of state-of-the-art office spaces. The Financial Plaza is reserved for banks, and financial institutions, and houses its own dining and retail plaza offerings.

![](https://opti.kafd.sa/globalassets/images/cards/slides/kafd-grand-mosque-1-2f540.jpg)

#### KAFD GRAND MOSQUE

An architectural marvel, created by Omrania and inspired by the desert rose native to the Arabian Peninsula, the Grand Mosque features a unique geometric structure built without a single pillar.
