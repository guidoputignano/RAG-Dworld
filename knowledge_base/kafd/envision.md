## A NEW STANDARD FOR SMART CITIES

KAFD’s smart city mission involves the district's automation, the rollout city management solutions and continuous enhancement of operational efficiency. Our approach ensures the provision of new services for KAFD’s community, while contributing to an unparalleled customer experience. From data centers and cutting-edge digital infrastructure, to artificial intelligence application, KAFD’s transformational journey into a smart city has begun.

## COMMITTED TO SUSTAINABILITY

Sustainability is at the core of KAFD’s urban planning and future-proofing. We are committed to leading on our environmental, social and governance responsibilities and have already undertaken several key milestones to foster sustainable development. From greenifying the district, deployment of an automated waste management system, to LEED certifying our buildings, KAFD will continue to contribute to the Kingdom’s vision of a sustainable future.

## THE FUTURE OF BUSINESS

An unparalleled networking environment, home to business leaders and economic powerhouses from across the region. KAFD welcomes a diverse set of industry clusters, with leading companies all converging in one destination. With advanced office spaces, smart city infrastructure, and on-location data centers highly grounded in sustainability and efficiency, KAFD is the number one destination for cutting edge and future-proof connectivity.
