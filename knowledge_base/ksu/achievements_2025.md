# Achievements in 2025

1

### Excellence and Academic Accreditation

- Full institutional accreditation from (2024- October 2031).
  
- International academic accreditation for the College of Business Administration programs from AACSB.
  
- International academic accreditation for the Doctor of Pharmacy (PharmD) program from ACPE.
  
- Ranked first in the local ranking within the National Digital Education Index 2025.

2

### Excellence in Activities and Events

- National Award for Cultural Work 2025 in the category of empowering volunteering work in educational institutions.
  
- KSU's theatre team awards in Rabat International Festival of University Theatre.
  
- Eight Medals at the Geneva International Exhibition of Inventions.
  
- College of Architecture and Planning wins first and second place in the Cityscape Global Competition.
  
- Winning first place in the Anti-Doping Awareness Global Competition at the 6th International Conference on Pharmacy and Toxicology (KSAPT) 2025.

3

### Medical City

- Launch of the digital platform for comprehensive patient services.
  
- Obtaining CARF accreditation (Centers for Rehabilitation International) for 2024.
  
- Ranked sixth globally in sleep medicine according to ScholarGBS.
  
- Winning the 2025 Environment Award in the Environmental Sustainability category.
  
- The University Dental Hospital entered the Guinness World Records as the largest dental clinic in the world.
  
- The Medical City was accredited as the first goverment-run healthcare institution in the Middle East by the United Nations.
  
- King Khalid University Hospital was ranked among the best healthcare centers in the Middle East for 2026 by Newsweek International.

4

### University Teams

**King Saud University won first place in the following competitions:**
  

* - 12th Saudi Universities Paddle Championship.
* - Saudi University Sports Federation Championship for Women's Handball.
* - Saudi University Sports Federation Championship for Esports.
* - Saudi University Sports Federation Championship for Women's Karate.
* - Saudi University Sports Federation Championship for Athletics for People with Disabilities.
* - Electronic Robotics Hackathon.
* - Saudi University Sports Federation Championship for Handball.
