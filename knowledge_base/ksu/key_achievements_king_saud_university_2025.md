# Key Achievements by King Saud University in 2025

![Shanghai Ranking](https://portal.ksu.edu.sa/sites/default/files/2026-04/shanghai.png)

Achieved **first place** among Arab and Saudi universities and **116th** globally.

Ranked **11th** globally in Engineering, **21st** in Pharmacy, and **37th** in Agricultural Sciences in the **ARWU 2025** rankings.

![QS](https://portal.ksu.edu.sa/sites/default/files/2026-04/QS.png)

Ranked **143rd** globally and **second** among Saudi universities in the QS 2026 rankings.

Achieved a distinguished scientific presence in the rankings of disciplines and research fields, ranking **33rd** globally in Petroleum Engineering and **48th** globally in Dentistry and Pharmacy.

![U.S. News](https://portal.ksu.edu.sa/sites/default/files/2026-05/u.s.news_.png)

According to the **U.S. News & World Report 2025** rankings, King Saud University ranked **146th** globally and achieved positions within the top gobally in :

* Toxicology and Pharmacy: **4**
* Plant and Animal Sciences: **8**
* Polymer Science: **10**
* Mathematics: **15**

![ARU](https://portal.ksu.edu.sa/sites/default/files/2026-04/aru.png)

Maintained its **first-place** ranking for the third consecutive year in the Times Higher Education Arab University Rankings **(ARU 2026)**.

![Times Higher Education](https://portal.ksu.edu.sa/sites/default/files/2026-04/the.png)

Ranked the**20th** position globally in the **Times Higher Education Interdisciplinary Science Rankings 2026**.

![Clarivate](https://portal.ksu.edu.sa/sites/default/files/2026-04/clarivate.png)

**Fourteen** of the university’s scientific journals are indexed in the **Web of Science** database according to the **ESCI, SCIE, and SSCI** indexes.
