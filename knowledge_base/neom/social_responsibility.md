## OUR MESSAGE

To inspire, enable and sustain widespread responsible practices and initiatives that accelerate socio-economic participation, elevate quality of life, and address stakeholders’ needs.

![](https://neom.scene7.com/is/image/neom/accelerate-progress?wid=1440&hei=809)

Our Tracks

## ACCELERATE PROGRESS

By accelerating and facilitating the growth of an ecosystem that enhances the participation and long-term sustainability of local entrepreneurship in society and increases the participation of non-profit organizations and increases their contributions on a large scale.

[Read more](/en-us/about/social-responsibility/accelerate-progress "Read more")

![](https://neom.scene7.com/is/image/neom/embedding-value?wid=1440&hei=809)

Our Tracks

## EMBEDDING VALUE

By promoting a culture that establishes the principles of awareness and responsibility and shifts behavior towards adopting social innovation and inspiring sustainable practices that enhance the development of societies in a sustainable manner.

[Read more](/en-us/about/social-responsibility/embbeding-value "Read more")

![](https://neom.scene7.com/is/image/neom/quality-of-life?wid=1440&hei=809)

Our Tracks

## ENHANCING QUALITY OF LIFE

By enabling responsible initiatives aimed at raising the quality of life for stakeholders, focusing on employment, education, health, sports, environmental safety and security, and developing talents and hobbies.

[Read more](/en-us/about/social-responsibility/enhancing-quality-of-life "Read more")

## NEOM SR Reports

NEOM SR reports helps to realize NEOM's commitment to social responsibility and the sustainable development of communities and the environment. Through a human-centered approach, NEOM is striving to create a global model for economic, social, and environmental sustainability to foster meaningful impact at scale.
