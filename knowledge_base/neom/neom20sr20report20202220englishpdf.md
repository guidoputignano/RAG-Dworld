NEOM’s SEVEN SENSES ACCELERATOR is an ongoing series
of initiatives at the heart of the Accelerating Progress track.
Structured with entrepreneurs in mind, this signature program drives
economic and social engagement within communities, creating the
groundwork to develop an ecosystem for local SMEs, freelancers,
and craftspeople in NEOM and Tabuk to become trusted vendors
for NEOM’s businesses in areas of construction and logistics, media
event management, culture and tourism.
The first completed initiatives of the series, NEOM SEVEN SENSES
ACCELERATOR, Workshops, and the Entrepreneurship Forum,
were formulated by identifying the gaps needed to connect local
businesspeople to the opportunities in NEOM and ensuring that
they were equipped with the tools, technology, training and skills to
deliver their products and services based on NEOM’s internationally-
aligned operating standards.
