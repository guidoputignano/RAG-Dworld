P R E S E R V A T I O N
Earlier in the year, we launched the HERFA+
initiative aiming to provide the local
community with a platform that nurtured and
spotlighted their remarkable craftsmanship.
With the support of the Dulani Business Center of the Social Development
Bank and the Council of NGOs in Tabuk, the initiative empowered local
artists through intensive training programs and incentives that offered them
the chance to finally see their skills flourish.
Their passion for preserving the region’s legacy through traditional
handicrafts was met with heartwarming excitement at a 5-day bazaar at
Tabuk’s Park Mall specially prepared to display their works and transform
their talents from mostly part-time hobbies to financially rewarding local
design brands that would then sustain their incomes and help them prosper.
Throughout the initiative’s run, and the exposure that the participating
artists benefited from in NEOM’s communities, during the Saudi National
Day, as well as in OXAGON, they amassed a total revenue of more
than 150,000 Saudi Riyals, kickstarting the careers that they have been
dreaming of.

NEOM SR REPORT 2021 / ROOTS
Perhaps the most vital aspect of quality education, and the feature that the
initiatives we take on focus on most, is equipping the mind with the tools
and ample room and freedom to express themselves. It is through the
learned habit of potent expression that the local youth can muster the
courage to choose and follow enriching paths persistently. Behind every
great leap — every single discovery — in our world, lies that crucial capacity
to accurately express achievement.
The initiatives that follow were a result of
our aim to give the local community a way
to express the brilliance of their aspirations
through an education that enriches them and
harmonizes with their vast potential.

NEOM SR REPORT 2021 / ROOTS
E S S E N T I A L S
In continuation of our past efforts through
the years in offering educational programs
that further develop the members of the local
community, the fifth iteration of the English
Language & Information Technology program
was held in cooperation with the Fahad bin
Sultan University.
The program consisted of a thorough six-week training course that
serves students living across the Tabuk region, from Sharma, Gayal, and
Al-Khuraybah, to Maqna and Al-Bad’.
Prior to the beginning of the course, placement tests were conducted
to evaluate students and place them in appropriate tracks specifically
designed to target areas of growth in developing their skills in the
English language and in information technology. Once placements were
determined, the six weeks that followed helped students sharpen their
abilities and unlock paths to employment opportunities within NEOM’s
sectors and partner companies.

NEOM SR REPORT 2021 / ROOTS
E X P R E S S I O N S
O F P O T E N T I A L
Taking the first step toward distinctive jobs that allow learned skills to
flourish is often an obstacle for many. Further empowering the local
community to make use of employment opportunities and advancing their
capabilities to adequately identify ways of expressing their potential to
employers, we organized a Resume Writing program that eased entry into
the workforce.
The initiative helped job seekers not only craft compelling and professional
resumes that better reflected their abilities and efforts, but also acquire the
knowledge to properly comprehend and analyze which elements employers
tend to be on the lookout for in such documents. By training them how to
better write their resumes and how to assess those of others, we hope that
they in turn transfer what they learned to the rest of the local community
who might have much to say about their abilities but may be unable to
effectively communicate that potential to others.
A robust platform for Careers at NEOM was also created to help better
provide the community with accessible paths to exciting job positions with
NEOM and our partners. Applicants are given the opportunity to undergo
specific training that advances their abilities and qualifies them for the jobs
they seek through the platform.
