Our Story

## 2019

##

NEOM Company is incorporated as a single-shareholder joint stock company.
  
  
NEOM Bay Airport becomes operational.

## 2020

##

NEOM commenced construction on essential infrastructure.
  
  
NEOM Green Hydrogen Company announced in an equal joint venture between Air Products, ACWA Power and NEOM.

## 2021

##

Plans to build our industrial city, Oxagon, are announced.
  
  
A Rare Biodiversity Program is established to document and conserve the natural heritage of the region.

## 2022

##

The port was handed to NEOM in August 2022, making it the first operational asset in Oxagon.

## 2023

##

The NEOM Green Hydrogen Company completes financial close.
  
  
Global shipping lines start to call at Port of NEOM.

## 2024

##

NEOM continued to build the enabling foundations for a new sustainable region, with major progress on enabling infrastructure across mobility, digital, energy and water.

## 2025

##

The NEOM Board of Directors announced the appointment of Eng. Aiman Al-Mudaifer as Managing Director and CEO of NEOM.

## Key regional projects

Positioned at the crossroads of global trade routes, NEOM combines a strategic location with current and planned infrastructure to connect markets and industries.

##

* ![Oxagon](/content/dam/neom/oxagon/oxagon-2025/241030_SO%2024_OXAGON%20Renderings%20Stage%20A%20Golden%20Updates_001%20(1).jpeg)

  ### Oxagon

  At the heart of regional progress is Oxagon, an emerging advanced industrial hub focused on clean technologies and anchored by the Port of NEOM. Strategically located on the Red Sea coast, it is attracting global and regional partners across renewable energy and AI-driven industries.

  Explore Oxagon
* ![THE LINE](/content/dam/neom/new-region/The%20Line.png)

  ### THE LINE

  Against a backdrop of extraordinary mountain, desert and marine landscapes, THE LINE represents a new model of urban development focused on livability, sustainability and innovation. A core component of NEOM’s long-term vision, THE LINE is advancing through a phased, demand-led approach.

  Explore THE LINE
* ![Trojena](https://neom.scene7.com/is/image/neom/TROJENA_DRONE_IMG_2022_Alblowy2?scl=1)

  ### Trojena

  Located within the mountain range of Jabal Al-Lawz, Trojena is a year-round mountain destination enjoying altitudes of up to 2,600 m above sea level. Northwest Saudi Arabia's highest peak, the mountain destination features subzero winter temperatures and panoramic views.

  Explore Trojena
* ![Nature](https://neom.scene7.com/is/image/neom/Nature-2?fmt=png-alpha&scl=1)

  ### NEOM NATURE RESERVE

  The NEOM Nature Reserve leads conservation efforts at NEOM and is delivering a conservation program focused on innovation and adaptive management. The Reserve currently operates a single wildlife reserve program covering an area larger than 19,000 km², including parts of the beautiful Bajdah desert.

  Explore Nature
* ![Nature](/content/dam/neom/new-region/Island%20of%20NEOM.jpg)

  ### Islands of NEOM

  At the crossroads of three continents, the Islands of NEOM are located within the rich marine ecosystem of the Red Sea. The surrounding waters are home to over a thousand fish and marine mammal species, more than 300 coral species, and hundreds of turtle nesting sites.

  Explore Islands of NEOM
* ![Nature](https://neom.scene7.com/is/image/neom/Islands?fmt=png-alpha&scl=1)

  ### MAGNA

  Located in the northwestern arm of the Red Sea, Magna stretches across Gulf of Aqaba’s dramatic coastline. The destination features exceptional access to natural beauty, where valleys, mountains, and sea come together to create a distinctive environment.

  Explore Magna
