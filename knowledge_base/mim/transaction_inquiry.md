# Inquire Your Transaction

To facilitate the process of following up on the progress and status of transactions electronically, the Ministry of Industry and Mineral Resources provides the Transaction Inquiry Service, which allows beneficiaries to view the most important data related to their transactions without the need to visit the Ministry's headquarters.
