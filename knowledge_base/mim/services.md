## Available Services

Grid displaying available services. Each service can be accessed for details or to make a request.

### Querying transactions

To facilitate the process of tracking the progress and status of transactions

[Service Request](/en/services/transaction-inquiry)

### Mining Licenses Transfer

This service allows the holder of a license (exploration or exploitation) to transfer their license to another eligible investor. This process is overseen by the Ministry of Industry and Mineral Resources to ensure compliance with legal, financial, and technical standards.

Mining Sector

[Service Details](/en/services/mining-licenses-transfer)[Service Request](https://taadeen.sa/ar/services/%D8%AA%D8%AD%D9%88%D9%8A%D9%84-%D8%B1%D8%AE%D8%B5%D8%A9)

### Renew Industrial License

Extends license validity for up to five years upon meeting all requirements.

Industrial Sector

[Service Details](/en/services/renew-industrial-license)[Service Request](https://industry.sa/ar/services/renew_upgrade_to_production)

### Customs Exemption for Equipment and Spare Parts

This service enables industrial investors to obtain a customs exemption for machinery, equipment, and spare parts after obtaining an industrial license.

Industrial Sector

[Service Details](/en/services/customs-exemption-equipment-spare-parts)[Service Request](https://industry.sa/ar/services/customs_spare_parts_machines)

### Transfer Ownership

An electronic service that enables industrial investors to permanently cancel their industrial licenses.

Industrial Sector

[Service Details](/en/services/transfer-ownership)[Service Request](https://industry.sa/ar/services/transfer_factory_ownership)

### Labor Enablement

This service enables industrial investors to obtain workforce approval based on the establishment’s labor needs, following an on-site assessment.

Industrial Sector

[Service Details](/en/services/Labor-Enablement)[Service Request](https://industry.sa/ar/services/labors_enablement)

### Unrestricted Chemical Imports Permit

This service allows industrial investors to request a permit for importing and clearing unrestricted chemicals necessary for production within the industrial facility. The request is evaluated by the Ministry of Industry and Mineral Resources in coordination with relevant authorities, in accordance with updated regulations.

Industrial Sector

[Service Details](/en/services/Unrestricted-Chemical-Imports-Permit)[Service Request](https://industry.sa/ar/services/non_restricted_chemical_permit)

### Cancel Industrial License

Allows the industrial investor to permanently cancel the industrial license.

Industrial Sector

[Service Details](/en/services/cancel-industrial-license)[Service Request](https://industry.sa/ar/services/license_cancellation)

### Mining information

Register of Applications:
All applications for obtaining licenses submitted to the Ministry are recorded in this register.
Register of Licenses:
All issued licenses are recorded in this register, along with any renewal, amendment, transfer, extension, mortgage, termination, expiry, or any other action that affects them.

Mining Sector

[Service Details](/en/services/Mining-information)[Get information](https://cms.taadeen.sa/ar/mining-info)
